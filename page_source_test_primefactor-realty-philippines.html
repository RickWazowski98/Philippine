Description
PRIMEFACTOR REALTY PHILIPPINES is a real estate organization that specializes in the field of industrial and commercial real estate brokerage for corporate clients. As an industrial real estate solutions provider, our expertise is geared towards optimizing opportunities by providing options from which you can choose warehouses, production facilities, vacant industrial or commercial land (both in general industrial areas or PEZA-designated export zones), and office spaces. Our company operates mainly in strategic areas located towards the south of Metro Manila (lower Quezon City, the cities of Pasig, Taguig, Paranaque and Muntinlupa) and the Cavite, Laguna and Batangas provinces.
